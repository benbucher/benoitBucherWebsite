Here is a few nonsense reveries.

The misty drawer

Find hidden memories
In the old house drawer.
Remember the tall trees,
May they live forever.

And all the dreams of you,
Come back again so true.
The time spent together,
Is a misty fever.

Crushed by the waves of time.
Entire is your crime.
Joy is like a gravel,
Trapped in your endless spell.

For that it never will
be any real again,
Is the source of all pain.
May it not keep you still,
Can it live in your soul forever?
