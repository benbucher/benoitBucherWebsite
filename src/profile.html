Hello, my name is Benoît and I am an aerospace engineer based in France

About

Born in Switzerland in 1991, I grew up and studied in France for most of my life. In 2015, I graduated with a master degree in aerospace engineering. Passionate about aerospace and aviation, I am currently working in the space division of a major player of the industry. Curious and eager to learn, I am interested in engineering, project management, digitalisation, finance and innovation.
More recently, I took a year off from work. During these months, I had more time for personal projects and learning on other topics. I got interested in acquiring web development skills, which resulted in building this website. I like to spend my free time in the mountains, with friends and family. Cinema, photography, a bit of running and reading books are some of my favorite hobbies.
